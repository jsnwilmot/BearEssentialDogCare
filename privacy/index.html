Privacy Policy

This policy explains how contact information is used when you contact Bear Essential Dog Care through this website, email, phone, or Facebook.

Information you may provide

You may choose to provide your name, phone number, email address, dog's name, breed or size, service needs, general location, preferred appointment timing, and message details.

How information is used

Information is used to respond to your inquiry, discuss mobile grooming needs, confirm service area, and arrange or follow up on appointments.

What this website does not do

This website does not publish form details publicly and does not include a payment checkout. The contact form prepares an email in your own email app.

Third-party links

The site links to Facebook and mail or phone services. Those services have their own privacy practices.

Retention

Contact details may be kept as needed to respond to inquiries and manage grooming appointments. You can ask Bear Essential Dog Care about your contact information by email.

Contact

For privacy questions, email tammy@bearessentials.ca or call (403) 634-9408.
