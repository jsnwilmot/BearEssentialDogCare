Mobile dog grooming services in Lethbridge.

Bear Essential Dog Care brings grooming to your home with one-on-one attention and no kenneling. Services are discussed before booking so Tammy can understand your dog's coat, size, comfort level, and grooming needs.

What can be included.

The current service list includes the following grooming care. No prices are listed here because appointments depend on the dog's needs and should be confirmed directly with Tammy.

Brush out

Coat brushing to help with tangles, loose hair, and a cleaner grooming finish.

Shampoo and conditioning

A bath and coat conditioning step as part of the mobile grooming appointment.

Hand blow dry

Hand drying in the mobile setup, with no kenneling or tethering stated on the current site.

Haircut or trim

Haircut or trim work based on coat condition, coat length, and what is comfortable for your dog.

Ears, nails, and pads

Care for ears, nails, and paw pads as part of a tidy and comfortable groom.

Full body massage

A gentle full body massage is included in the current service list.

A clear note about anal glands.

Bear Essential Dog Care does not express anal glands and recommends this be handled by a veterinarian.

Not sure what your dog needs?

Call or email Tammy with your dog's name, breed or size, coat condition, and any comfort concerns. She can explain what may be suitable before you book.
