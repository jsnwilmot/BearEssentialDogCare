About Tammy, Bear, and the Spa on Wheels.

Bear Essential Dog Care is owner-operated by Tammy Himsl in Lethbridge, Alberta. The business was inspired by Bear, Tammy's dog, and by the kind of care dogs deserve when grooming feels stressful or unfamiliar.

A dog named Bear started the story.

The current Bear Essential site shares how Tammy's love for Bear helped lead her into pet first aid, grooming training, and eventually mobile dog care.

That story still sits at the heart of the business: dogs give their families unconditional love, and Bear Essential helps local owners return that love with calm, practical grooming care.

The mobile setup means Tammy can focus on one dog at a time, without kennel waiting or a busy shop environment.

Care with a local touch.

Bear Essential Dog Care is not built around rushing dogs through a line. It is built around a simple idea: bring the grooming spa to the home and make the experience easier for the dog and owner.
