Questions about mobile dog grooming.

Short answers for Lethbridge dog owners before contacting Bear Essential Dog Care.

Do you groom dogs at my home?

Yes. Bear Essential Dog Care is a mobile dog grooming service. Tammy brings the grooming spa to your home inside the Lethbridge city limits.

What areas do you serve?

The current service area is the city limits of Lethbridge, Alberta. If you are unsure whether your location is included, call or email Tammy.

How long does a groom take?

Grooming time depends on coat condition, coat length, dog size, and how comfortable the dog is with grooming. Tammy can discuss timing when you ask about booking.

Will my dog be kenneled?

No. The current site describes one-on-one care with no kenneling or tethering.

What services can be included?

Services can include brush out, shampoo and conditioning, hand blow dry, haircut or trim, ears, nails, pads, and full body massage.

Do you express anal glands?

No. Bear Essential Dog Care recommends anal gland expression be handled by a veterinarian.

What happens in bad weather?

Extreme weather or poor road conditions may affect appointments. Call Bear Essential Dog Care to discuss options if weather is a concern.

How can I help my dog prepare for grooming?

Have your dog ready at home, share any coat issues or handling concerns, and let Tammy know if your dog is nervous around grooming, paws, ears, or drying.

How do I book or ask a question?

Call (403) 634-9408, email tammy@bearessentials.ca, or use the contact page to prepare an email with your dog's details.

Still have a question?

Send Tammy your dog's name, breed or size, service needs, and general Lethbridge location.
